About Founder

Mr. Abrham Mathai, the founder of Amstor, is an entrepreneur with a vision of transforming technology into a business revolution across various fields. With a strong background in software development, engineering consultancy, and IT consultancy, Mr. Mathai embarked on his career in pharmaceuticals at the age of 24 by investing 1.5 Crores and setting up the Dio-Pharma Laboratories, in Gujarat, at the young yet prime age of 24 after graduating in Economics. After gaining years of international experience in the USA, Singapore, and the Middle East, he founded Amstor in 1996 as a pioneer member of Technopark Trivandrum, which has now become the fastest-growing IT hub in South India. Recognizing the potential in the healthcare, hospitality, and IT sectors, Mr. Mathai expanded his business to the Middle East to cater to customers in those industries. With his visionary leadership, Amstor continues to deliver innovative solutions and drive digital transformation in the ever-evolving business landscape.
