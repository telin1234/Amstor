Patient web portal

Patient Portal is an advanced web portal specially designed to serve the patients to access their personal profile, medical reports, booking appointments, result access.etc. Patients can access it through any web enable devices.  

Patient portal provides the patients easy access and interaction facility with the health facilities to get the best service experience.

Patient portal enables the patients to access the system through the clinic/medical center websites and can book the appointment based on the available slots of each doctor. Also, it facilitates to make the online payment, Report/Result access.
