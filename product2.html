LeHealth HIS Day Surgical Centers

“A fully Integrated Web-based Application for managing Dental Specialty
based Clinics and Centers in UAE & GCC Countries.”

LeHealth HIS Provides a complete operations management solution for small, medium and large level Medical Day Surgery Centres. The Centralized architecture of the software allows healthcare management team to handle single or multichain facilities easy and effective.

LeHealth HIS is a fully integrated, advanced web-based healthcare information system (EMR System), specifically designed to manage the complete operations of Day Surgery Centers and Medical Centers. LeHealth offers an unmatched flexibility to choose and customize the operational requirements of the healthcare facilities based on the current and future requirements. Latest and advanced user interface of the system offers best experience and support to the users to manage their day-to-day operations easy and effective, hence assuring the best patient experience and satisfaction. LeHealth HIS solution offers best in class centralized Day Surgical Center management system for small, medium and large level single or multichain enterprises

About LeHealth HIS

LeHealth HIS offers an advanced operations management system for Medical Day Surgical Centers and Medical Centers in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Al Ain, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah, UAE and other GCC countries. The web-based application offers flexible option to choose the deployment method of the system asper customer choice of interest (On-premise server or cloud serve deployment). The Modular structure of the system allows the facilities to select the features and functionalities based on their specialties and requirements. Latest Technology and Graphical User Interface (GUI) of the system offers greater experience to the end users to learn it quick and use it with ease, faster & quality. LeHealth, holds the pride of one of the long serving and best preferred HIS software solution, providing specialty based dedicated software solutions for Medical Clinics, Medical centers and Day Surgical Centers in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Al Ain, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah and Fujairah, UAE and other GCC countries.

Amstor LeHealth system meets with current guidelines of Ministry of Health (MOH), Dubai Health Authority (DHA) and specific requirements of all major insurance companies.

LeHealth HIS system facilitates to manage both outpatient and inpatient management to meet the standard operational requirements of Day surgical centers. The system offers to manage the functionalities like, IP admission, bed management, Room or ward management, transfer or discharge of patients. Also facilitate to schedule and track the operation booking, Theatre booking, completion status..etc
