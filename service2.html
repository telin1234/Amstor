Standard accounting/ERP Software
Integration

LeHealth Software offers the capability to integrate the software with any global standard accounting software or ERP system to achieve the data sharing to make the booking keeping Up to Date. The integrations are done through the API integrations or DB integrations. The seamless integrations can facilitate whole solutions to work as single platform to produce desired result faster and accurate.

Lehealth captures all accounts related daily billing, Outstanding, advances, receipts & payment, stock management and Item consumptions etc., thus it facilitates to transfer this information directly to the third-party accounting or ERP system through integration to maintain the bookkeeping process up-to-date. This helps the management to save the valuable time and get the information required information on their finger-tips on-time and any time verification.
