About our company

We’re delivering the best
customer experience

Amstor Technologies LLC is one of the best health care information management system providers for polyclinic and medical centers all across UAE and GCC countries. Amstor is a fast-growing software development company headquartered in Dubai, UAE with a state-of-the art software Development centre based in Trivandrum Techno Park providing cutting-edge web-based clinic management system (EMR) under the brand name of Amstor LeHealth.

LeHealth Products

“A fully integrated web-based application for managing all types of medical clinics and medical centers in UAE & GCC countries”

LeHealth provides a complete operations management solution for small, medium & large level Poly clinics, Medical centers, Medical Laboratories & Diagnostic Centres. The centralized architecture of the software allows healthcare management team to handle single or multi-chain facility easy and effective.
